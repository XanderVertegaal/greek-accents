Accentuation of the definite article

The definite article follows three rules:

  • The direct cases (nom./acc.) are oxytone: τόν, τώ, τά. (A1)
    • Exception: The nom./acc. sg./pl. m./f. are toneless: ὁ, ἡ, οἱ, αἱ. (A1a)
  • A2:The oblique cases (gen./dat.) are perispomenon: τῶν, τοῖν, ταῖς. (A2)
