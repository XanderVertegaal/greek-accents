Accentuation of the definite article

The accentuation of the definite article follows three rules:

  1. The direct cases (nom./acc.) are oxytone: τόν, τώ, τά etc. (A1)
    • Exception: The nom./acc. sg./pl. m./f. are toneless: ὁ, ἡ, οἱ, αἱ. (A1a)
  2. The oblique cases (gen./dat.) are perispomenon: τῶν, τοῖν, ταῖς etc. (A2)

NB: the vocative marker ὦ (not strictly an article) is perispomenon.
